"Sanctum Library"is a modern way library with better ui/ux and easy on-the-go learning. It provides easy-format content like pdf, web links and YT videos for efficient learning.

You can login/register your account with login button in navbar, and after logging in you can see a navigation button to your account from where you can monitor your progress in overall learning and courses.

You can make changes to your profile from "My Account" page, where you can see an option to update profile. You can upload new profile picture to update it all over "Sanctum Library".

You can change password from My account > update profile page, incase you dont remember your old password you can contact us using our social media handles.

"Sanctum E-library" is a online library management system made for an old classic library. User can apply for registration and admin/librarian will approve them after all procedure success.

It is your credit/money, that you can use to issue books from "Sanctum E-Library". You can gain or lose more by/through contacting librarian.
